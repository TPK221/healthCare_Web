Frequently Asked
Questions

What are the visiting hours?
The visiting hours are from 9am to 5pm (Monday-Friday). Weekends (Saturday & Sunday) are from 9am to 9pm.
Can visitors stay overnight?
Only one visitor is allowed to stay overnight with the patient. Please visit the hospital office for an official overnight pass.
Can I park my car at the medical centre?
Yes, you can park your car at our hospital parking lot located at Lower Ground 1 and Lower Ground 2.
What if I can't attend my appointment?
If you cannot keep your appointment, or you need to change the time and date, please telephone the number on your appointment letter as soon as possible. This will allow us to rearrange your appointment time and to give yours to someone else.
