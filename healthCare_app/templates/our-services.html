Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation Services

We provide this service if patients are recovering from surgery, healing from an injury, or being treated for a disabling health problem.

Many health problems that cause pain or limit your ability to move normally may be helped by physical therapy. Here are some health problems that it may help:

Stroke. Stroke is a block or burst of the blood supply to the brain. The most important part of stroke recovery is early rehab that often starts right away in the hospital. Rehab can help you relearn how to manage daily activities. It can also help you get back lost function and prevent more damage.

Spine injury. Neck and back injuries need early treatment for the best results. Physical therapy and rehab for these injuries start as soon as possible at the hospital. They help limit more damage and shorten recovery time.

Pain. If you have severe pain in the hospital after an injury or an operation or because of a nerve or muscle problem, physical therapy may help ease your pain. It can also help you to better handle it.

Joint conditions. Joint problems can cause pain, stiffness, and limited movement. They may be treated with physical therapy in the hospital. Therapy can ease pain and improve range of movement. It can also teach you how to use your joints without causing more damage. Examples of these conditions are ankylosing spondylitis, osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and gout.

Home Nursing Services

We believe that everyone has the right to live within the comforts of their own home, regardless of their health care needs. For those with complex care needs, private nurses oversee your care from the very beginning of your time with us. Their responsibilities include:

Completing an initial carers assessment with you alongside a local live-in care manager.

Working closely with other medical professionals involved in your care, such as social workers.

Finding a private carer that has the relevant skills and experience in your care needs.

Providing bespoke training to a chosen carer if they need upskilling for your specific health condition.

Contributing to a fully comprehensive support plan that includes medication, health condition(s), personal care, and hobbies & interests.

Visits to check that your care plan is up to date and to see how you are.

Mental Health Care Services

We support people with mental health problems with a psychologist, therapist and a counsellor. TYK Medical Centre provides many evidence-based treatments that can help you to overcome the difficulties you’ve been experiencing and set you on a path to improve your wellbeing in the long term. Our treatment programmes are delivered by a world-class team of therapists, psychiatrists and psychologists at our network of Malaysia hospital sites and wellbeing centres. Tailored to your needs, each programme can be scaled up or down depending on the level of support needed for your recovery.

Treatment programmes with us include:

Inpatient (residential)

For the most intensive treatment, you can stay at one of our mental health hospitals on a residential basis. Here, you can receive round-the-clock care, expert therapy, wellbeing activities and other effective treatments that puts the focus on your recovery.  In inpatient treatment, you’re able to recover away from the stresses of normal life and in an environment that’s specifically designed for recovery from mental health disorders.

Day care and outpatient

This is when you attend a hospital site or wellbeing centre for a set number of days, half-days or hourly sessions to receive mental health treatment and therapy. This type of treatment is flexible and allows you to work on your recovery around some of your other responsibilities at home and work.

Dental Services

TYK Medical Centre offers an extensive range of treatments and expertise across our network of 400+ practices. Our dentists provide everything from general check-ups and cosmetic dentistry, to more complex treatments such as dental implants and oral surgery.

We also provide specialist care, including periodontists (experts in gum disease), endodontists (specialists in complex root canal) and dentists that are highly experienced in dealing with nervous or anxious patients.

Our treatments include fillings, crowns. veneers, implants, root canal, dentures, dental bridges.
